Have you ever seen an item called a Magicshot while hunting? Or have you ever wondered about what a Magicshot is while reading the detailed information about a weapon?
A Magicshot is an item that increases your magic attack. Directions for usage are identical to Soulshots. While Soulshots increase physical damage, Magicshots increase magic attack powers.
When using Magicshots, your spellcasting speed increases, as well as the damage and the success rate of debuff type magic. It is quite a useful item.
You will be able to acquire Magicshot-using weapons when you reach level 15. Check the information when buying a weapon. If the Magicshot consumption is more than x1, it means that the weapon uses as many Magicshots as indicated.
