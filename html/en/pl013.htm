You may raise the level of the clan. You will need the following:
Level 1 SP: 30,000, Adena: 650,000
Level 2 SP: 150,000, Adena: 2,500,000
Level 3 SP: 500,000, Item: Proof of Blood
Level 4 SP: 1,400,000, Item: Proof of Determination
Level 5 SP: 3,500,000, Item: Proof of Anticipation
You may obtain the Proof of Blood by hunting the bloody queen in Dragon Valley.
