In order to become an Elven Knight or an Elven Ranger, you must be at least level 20, and you must pass a test.
However, your occupation will not actually change while you are here in the Elven Forest. You must journey out into the Human Kingdom. Gludio, which lies south of the forest, is relatively near.
You will receive further counsel from Grand Master Rains of the Fighters Guild in Gludio Castle Town.Return
