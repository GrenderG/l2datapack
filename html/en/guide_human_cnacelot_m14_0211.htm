You've probably noticed that Mystic and Fighter weapons are sold separately. Even if they seem to be of the the same type, there are subtle differences.
Fighter weapons cause heavy damage but possess low magic attack power, while with Mystic weapons the ratio is reversed. However, Fighter weapons do have some weak magical ability, and if you're a Mystic with a taste for hand-to-hand combat, you might choose to wield a sword instead of a staff.
