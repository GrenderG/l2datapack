Mercenary Red Foot:
In Giran Castle Town, there is a young man whose only aim in life is to take revenge. Everybody has been telling him that it’s useless but he is determined to kill the earthdragon Antharas with his own hands. Every day, he makes special arrows. Furthermore, if anybody brings him the raw materials he needs to make the arrows, he will pay the person with an ample reward. If you are interested, why don’t you go see him? His name is Belton and he works as a guard in Giran Castle Town.
