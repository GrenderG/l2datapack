Skills
1) Active skills : These are skills that show their effects only when used.
2) Passive skillls : These are skills that demonstrate an effect continuously from the moment of their acquisition.

Actions
1) Sit down/stand up : You can toggle between sit and stand up modes by clicking on the icon.
2) Walk/run : You can toggle between walk and run modes by clicking on the icon.
3) Attack : The target is attacked if you click the icon.
4) Forced attack : You can change to PK mode and back by clicking on the icon.
5) Exchange : If you click the icon after selecting the party to trade with, the exchange window is activated.
