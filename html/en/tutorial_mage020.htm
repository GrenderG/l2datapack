Skills

Congratulations! You have reached level 7!
As a Mystic, you may learn new skills from Sorcerers when you reach level 7. The Sorcerers who teach you skills are marked in yellow, so go speak to them.
Remember, to learn skills you will need skill points (SP). You may check your skill points (SP) from your Status window (ALT+T). If you do not have enough SP for the skill you want, obtain more by hunting. Your SP and experience will increase as you successfully hunt.
