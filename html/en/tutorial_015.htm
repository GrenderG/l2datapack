Map & Radar

The radar is a useful tool that allows you to locate party members or monsters while hunting. The dot on the center is your current location, and selected characters are marked in red. 'N' stands for North, 'S' for South, 'W' for West, and 'E' for East.
The map gives you a wider view for locating your position or moving to other areas. Double-click on the map in the Item window to display the Map window. The cursor and the directional arrow on the map represent your location and the direction you are headed.
