You may have heard of a Magicshot while you were hunting or possibly read a detailed description of it.
A Magicshot increases your magic attack. Its usage is identical to Soulshots. While Soulshots increase damage inflicted by physical attack, Magicshots increase the strength of magic attacks.
When using Magicshots, your spellcasting speed increases, as well as the damage and the success rate of debuff type magic. It can prove to be a very useful item.
You may acquire Magicshot-using weapons when you reach level 15. Check for the instructions when you buy weapons. If the Magicshot consumption is more than x1, that weapon uses the number of Magicshots indicated.
