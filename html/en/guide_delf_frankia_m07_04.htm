When you reach Level 7, you will learn new magic. Seek instruction from the Sorcerors of the Temple of Shilen. The Temple of Shilen is the largest building in the village, located on the north side.
You have probably noticed by now that when you defeat monsters you gain SP and experience. SP means "skill points," and they are deducted whenever you learn new skills or magic. You can check your current SP by clicking on the Character's Status button, then on the Status tab.
